Hi, I'm Aarthi. I design and ship product UX, end to end.

My design journey started with a challenge I chose to enter — and won. That win turned into an internship.

Early on, I worked at a design agency that built both products and services — which threw me into a wide range of problems and taught me to adapt fast. That breadth still shapes how I work: comfortable moving between research, systems and high-fidelity craft, across whatever a problem needs.

Lately I've been folding AI deeper into my work — not just in my design process, but by building my own small software with it: a password manager, an expense tracker, and a handful of experiments. Designing and shipping my own tools keeps me close to how things actually get built.

And when I'm not designing, I'm usually making something else — gardening, drawing, reading, or crafting. Creativity isn't just for screens.
