I'm Aarthi, a Senior UI/UX Designer bridging the gap between user empathy and technical execution.

7 years ago, I took a chance on a design challenge, won it, and never looked back. Since then, I've been helping companies turn complex problems into intuitive digital products.

Growing up in a fast-paced agency environment taught me to adapt quickly across the entire product lifecycle. Today, I move fluidly between deep user research, scalable design systems, and high-fidelity execution. I don't just design screens; I design the systems that make them work.

Lately, I've been exploring the intersection of design and AI. Not just using it as a tool, but coding my own small software with it: a password manager, an expense tracker, and a few experiments.

When I'm offline, I'm usually gardening, drawing, or reading. Because keeping creativity alive off-screen is what fuels my best work on it.
